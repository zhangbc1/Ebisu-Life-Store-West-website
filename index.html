ABOUT EBISU

Our History

Ebisu Life Store was founded by a team passionate about Japanese retail and lifestyle. Starting in local North American communities, we grew alongside the increasing demand for authentic Japanese snacks and goods. We have steadily refined our supply chain to bring the most suitable and exciting products to local customers.

Partnership

Ebisu operates through a "Direct Store + Diverse Cooperation" model. While our core stores are team-operated, we welcome quality suppliers and brands for long-term partnerships and co-branded events. We value transparent communication and data-driven feedback to optimize product selection for mutual long-term value.
